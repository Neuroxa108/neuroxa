HealGuard

HealGuard is a comfortable and easy-to-use wound dressing belt designed to simplify your healing process. Ideal for burns, ulcers, injuries.

HealGuard prioritizes your comfort with special gauze that won't stick to your wound, making dressing changes less painful. Soft, protective layers add an extra layer of comfort, while adjustable Velcro straps ensure a secure and personalized fit, allowing you to move with ease.

Steps

Cleanse

Gently clean the wound and surrounding skin using a mild antiseptic solution and pat the area dry.

Medicate

Apply the provided medicated ointment to cover the wound area.

Position

Carefully position the HealGuard belt so that it directly covers the wound. Ensure complete coverage

Secure

Adjust the Velcro straps to achieve a snug but comfortable fit.

About us

Neuroxa Mediproducts was born from a personal need for better wound care. Frustrated by the limitations of traditional dressings, our founder, Ayush Rana, envisioned a more effective and comfortable solution.

Guided by mentors from prestigious institutions like IIT and supported by the innovation ecosystem of Anand Pharmacy College, this vision became a reality with the development of the HealGuard Wound Dressing Belt. Today, Neuroxa is driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to making high-quality healthcare accessible to all. We are constantly researching and developing new solutions to address unmet medical needs, transforming healthcare one innovation at a time.
